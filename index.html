How To Make a Smoothie

A step-by-step guide

  1. Step 1: Gather Ingredients
  2. Step 2: Prepare Ingredients
  3. Step 3: Blend & Serve

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

Gather your favorite fruits and vegetables

Step 2: Prepare Ingredients

Cut your fruits and/or vegetables and place them into your blender with milk or water

Step 3: Blend & Serve

Blend all of your ingredients until smooth and enjoy!
